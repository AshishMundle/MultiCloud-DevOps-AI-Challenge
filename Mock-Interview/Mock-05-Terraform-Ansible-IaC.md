## ❓ 20 Questions — Terraform & Ansible IaC

### 🟢 Basic (Q1–Q5)
1. Walk me through your experience with IaC.
2. What is Terraform & Ansible IaC and why does it matter for modern DevOps?
3. Explain the main components of Terraform & Ansible IaC in simple terms.
4. What is the first thing you check if Terraform & Ansible IaC fails?
5. How do you stay current on best practices for Terraform & Ansible IaC?

### 🔵 Intermediate (Q6–Q10)
6. Describe a real project where you implemented Terraform & Ansible IaC.
7. How do you handle security and access control in Terraform & Ansible IaC?
8. Walk through setting up Terraform & Ansible IaC from scratch on a new server.
9. What is your structured approach to troubleshooting Terraform & Ansible IaC in production?
10. How does Terraform & Ansible IaC integrate with the broader CI/CD pipeline?

### 🟡 Scenario-Based (Q11–Q15)
11. **Scenario:** Production down — logs point to Terraform & Ansible IaC issue. Your action plan?
12. **Scenario:** Junior engineer misconfigured Terraform & Ansible IaC. Traffic impacted. What do you do?
13. **Scenario:** Management wants 30% cost reduction in Terraform & Ansible IaC infrastructure. Your approach?
14. **Scenario:** Migrate current Terraform & Ansible IaC setup to new architecture with zero downtime plan.
15. **Scenario:** Security vulnerability found in your Terraform & Ansible IaC pipeline. Remediation steps?

### 🔴 Senior / Architect Level (Q16–Q20)
16. Design Terraform & Ansible IaC for multi-region high availability targeting 99.99% SLA.
17. What monitoring and alerting strategy do you implement for Terraform & Ansible IaC?
18. Describe your disaster recovery experience involving Terraform & Ansible IaC.
19. How do you mentor junior engineers on Terraform & Ansible IaC best practices?
20. Top 3 mistakes teams make with Terraform & Ansible IaC and how you prevent each one.
